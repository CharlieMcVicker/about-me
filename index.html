About me

I am a young software developer living in Southern VT. I just finished my freshman year at Union College, in Scenectady, where I study math, computer science, philosophy and German. My personal interests are in theory of mind, translation, continental philosphy, metaphysics, ontology, and the applications of math and computer science, as well as the benefits of living at the intersection of the two, within these topics.

Outside of school, I like to study ontotheology and theology in general, especially in regards recovering a meaninful relationship with place in nonagricultural communities.

Beyond that, I like to play the guitar, study bio-dynamic farming, and stand with a sign in my local roundabout.
